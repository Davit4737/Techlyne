Controls and shortcuts
B Buy market, S sell market. Works inside chart fullscreen too.
G toggles chart fullscreen. Use the Fullscreen button if the browser blocks the hotkey first.
Space pause / resume. Step advances one simulated tick when paused.
F toggles auto-follow.
C cancels all working limits. To exit a position, send the opposite Buy/Sell order.
Ctrl+Z undoes the latest drawing.
1-5 swap tools: cursor, trendline, rectangle, h-line, eraser.
Ctrl+Click places a limit at the cursor. Left click buys, right click sells.
Alt+Click cancels the nearest limit order line.
Wheel zooms the x-axis. Shift+Wheel zooms the y-axis.
Drag pans along time. Shift+Drag pans price vertically.
Pinch zooms x on touch devices. Two-finger drag pans y.
+ and - change order quantity quickly.
Set the opening tape
Pick the starting price, trading engine, motion profile, and order-flow model. Sandbox is unlimited. Dollar Account reveals cash and leverage controls. MNQ-style notional stays: 1 contract = current price × $2; leverage only reduces required margin.
Sandbox mode: unlimited buying power. Dollar Account mode: max contracts are based on equity, MNQ notional, and selected leverage.
Dollar Account uses simple margin math: required margin per contract = (current price × $2) ÷ leverage. Real broker margin can be different, but this keeps quantity from becoming fake casino chips.
