Controls and shortcuts
B Buy market, S sell market.
Space pause / resume. Step advances one simulated tick when paused.
F toggles auto-follow.
C cancels all working limits. X flattens at market.
Ctrl+Z undoes the latest drawing.
1-5 swap tools: cursor, trendline, rectangle, h-line, eraser.
Ctrl+Click places a limit at the cursor. Left click buys, right click sells.
Alt+Click cancels the nearest limit order line.
Wheel zooms the x-axis. Shift+Wheel zooms the y-axis.
Drag pans along time. Shift+Drag pans price vertically.
Pinch zooms x on touch devices. Two-finger drag pans y.
+ and - change order quantity quickly.
