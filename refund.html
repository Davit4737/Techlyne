Refund Policy.

Last updated: July 2026

Every BizAssist plan starts with a 7-day free trial, then runs on simple, no-lock-in monthly billing. The free trial is your risk-free window to decide. This page explains exactly how trials, refunds, and cancellations work.

1. Your 7-day free trial

Every new business gets 7 days of full access, free. This is your chance to try BizAssist with no risk. If it isn't right for you, cancel any time before the trial ends and you won't be charged a cent. Your subscription only begins, and your first payment is only taken, once the 7-day trial is over and you haven't cancelled.

2. Cancel anytime, no lock-in

There are no contracts or minimum terms. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard or by emailing us. When you cancel a paid subscription, you keep access until the end of the billing period you already paid for. We don't cut you off early, and we don't charge you again after that.

3. Refunds after the free trial

Because the 7-day free trial gives you a full, no-cost window to evaluate BizAssist before any money changes hands, payments are non-refundable once billing begins. Each monthly payment covers the service running live for your business during that period. If you don't want to be billed again, cancel before your next renewal date and you won't be charged going forward. Payments already made are not refunded.

4. Billing errors and duplicate charges

The above doesn't cover genuine mistakes. If you were charged in error, charged twice, or charged after you'd already cancelled, email us with your account details and we'll investigate and refund any incorrect charge in full.

5. Plan changes

If you switch between Standard and Pro mid-cycle, we'll prorate the difference on your next invoice rather than charging or refunding immediately.

6. How to cancel or raise a billing issue

You can cancel any time from the Subscription tab in your dashboard. For billing questions or to report a charge that looks wrong, email hello@bizzassist.xyz with your business name and the email used to sign up. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Continued use after an update means you accept the new policy.
