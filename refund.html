Refund Policy.

Last updated: July 2026

BizAssist runs on simple, no-lock-in monthly billing. This page explains exactly how refunds and cancellations work.

1. Cancel anytime, no lock-in

There are no contracts or minimum terms. You can cancel your subscription at any time. When you cancel, you keep access until the end of the billing period you already paid for — we don't cut you off early, and we don't charge you again after that.

2. 14-day money-back guarantee (first subscription only)

If you're a first-time subscriber and BizAssist isn't a fit within your first 14 days, email us and we'll refund that first payment in full — no questions asked. This guarantee applies once per business, to your first paid period only.

3. After the 14-day window

Once the 14-day guarantee period has passed, payments for the current billing period are non-refundable, since the service has already been running for your business during that time. You can still cancel at any point to stop future charges.

4. Billing errors and duplicate charges

If you were charged in error, charged twice, or charged after you'd already cancelled, email us with your account details and we'll investigate and refund any incorrect charge in full.

5. Plan changes

If you switch between Standard and Pro mid-cycle, we'll prorate the difference on your next invoice rather than charging or refunding immediately.

6. How to request a refund or cancel

Email hello@bizzassist.xyz with your business name and the email used to sign up. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Continued use after an update means you accept the new policy.
