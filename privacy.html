Privacy Policy.

Last updated: July 2026

BizAssist is currently in beta. This policy describes our practices today and may be updated as the product grows. We'll post material changes on this page.

1. Who we are

BizAssist ("we", "us") provides an AI front-desk assistant for service businesses. You can reach us any time at hello@bizzassist.xyz.

2. What we collect

  • Waitlist details: the email address you submit to request early access.
  • Chat messages: messages you type into the demo assistant, which are sent to our AI provider to generate a reply.
  • Basic technical data: standard information your browser sends (such as IP address), used to keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

3. How we use it

  • To contact you about early access and product updates.
  • To operate the AI assistant and improve its answers.
  • To protect the service against spam and abuse.

4. AI processing

Messages sent to the assistant are processed by a third-party AI provider (Anthropic) solely to generate a response. We do not sell your data or use it to build advertising profiles.

5. Sharing

We share data only with the service providers that help us run BizAssist (for example, our hosting and email-capture tools) and only as needed to operate the service. We never sell your personal information.

6. Data retention

We keep waitlist emails until you ask us to remove them. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@bizzassist.xyz.

7. Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Just email us and we'll take care of it.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@bizzassist.xyz.
