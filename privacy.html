Privacy Policy.

Last updated: July 2026

This policy describes how we handle personal data today and may be updated as the product grows. We'll post material changes on this page.

1. Who we are

BizAssist provides an AI front-desk assistant for service businesses. Reach out any time at hello@bizzassist.xyz.

2. Two kinds of people this covers

This policy covers both the business owners who sign up for BizAssist and the customers of those businesses who chat with the assistant. For a business's own customers, the business decides what data is collected and why. BizAssist processes that data on the business's behalf to run their assistant.

3. What we collect

  • Account details: the name, email, business details, hours, services, and staff you provide when you sign up and configure your assistant.
  • Chat messages: messages typed into the assistant (on the demo or on a client's site), which are sent to our AI provider to generate a reply.
  • Booking details: when the assistant takes a booking, the customer's name, phone, email, and appointment details, so the business can fulfil the appointment and send reminders.
  • Payment data: when you subscribe, payment is handled by our payment provider; we receive billing status and limited details, not your full card number.
  • Technical data: standard information your browser sends (such as IP address), used to keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

4. How we use it

  • To operate the AI assistant: answering questions, booking appointments, and sending reminders.
  • To create and manage your account, subscription, and billing.
  • To contact you about your account and important product updates.
  • To keep the service secure and protect it against spam and abuse.

5. Sharing

Data is shared only with the trusted providers that power BizAssist behind the scenes (AI, scheduling, payments, hosting, and email), and only as needed to run the service. We never sell your personal information or use it to build advertising profiles.

6. Data retention

We keep account and booking data for as long as your account is active and as needed to run the service. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@bizzassist.xyz. If you're a customer of a business using BizAssist, contact that business, or reach out to us and we'll help.

7. Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Just email us and we'll take care of it.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@bizzassist.xyz.
